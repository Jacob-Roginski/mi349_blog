About Jacob Roginski

Hi! I'm Jacob, a writer and maker who loves finding the long off-the-beaten-path and using my longboard to explore new places. I write about how people can find adventure in everyday life through curiosity, simple routines, and practical creativity. My approach is hands-on: try something small, observe, and iterate.

When I'm not writing, I enjoy playing golf with friends, doing all the random activities that life throws at me. I believe that meaningful change often starts with tiny, consistent actions rather than sweeping resolutions. This site collects short essays and practical tips for people who want to build better days without adding friction.

Quick facts

  • Preferred travel: Walking or Longboarding
